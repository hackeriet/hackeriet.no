Welcome to Hackeriet!

Hackeriet is a hackerspace in Oslo. Hackerspaces are community-operated physical places, where people share their interest in tinkering with technology, meet and work on their projects, and learn from each other.

We reside on the first floor of Hausmannsgate 34, Oslo.

Upcoming events

When is it open?

Hackeriet is open for everyone Tuesdays and Saturdays from early in the evening till late. The front door isn't always open, in that case ring the door bell

Members have access around the clock. Right now there's no one there.

How can I join?

First, show up at a couple of open events so we can get to know each other. You don't have to be a member to be included, it's important to us that everyone feels welcome.

In addtion to paying dues members get to subscribe the internal e-mail list and a key to the space.

When you decide Hackeriet is for you, you fill out a short form and we take care of the details.

If you like to chat, join our IRC channel.
